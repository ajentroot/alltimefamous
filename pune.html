The Movie and Me

A psychological mystery directed by Tanveer Khan.
Produced by Anil Sharma.

I played the mysterious 'Aman'.

After her sister is killed in the 9/11 attacks, Anupama is devastated. When her fiancé Arpit leaves for America on business, Anu encounters a mysterious stranger named Aman. When Arpit returns, Anu tells him that she is in love with Aman and she can no longer marry him. But when the distraught Arpit demands, 'Who is this Aman?', there is no evidence to support her answers. Does Aman even exist...? "Madhoshi marked the directorial debut of Tanveer Khan who wove an intricate love story out of a great tragedy."
